About Ahmiyat Blockchain

Ahmiyat Blockchain utilizes the innovative "Proof of Memories" consensus algorithm.

What is Proof of Memories?

Unlike traditional proof-of-work blockchains that waste energy on meaningless calculations, Ahmiyat rewards users for contributing meaningful content in the form of memories - images, videos, memes, and text that have cultural or personal value.

How it Works

When you upload a memory, the system evaluates its uniqueness and assigns it a mining difficulty. Memories that require more effort to create and are more unique earn higher rewards. This creates a blockchain full of interesting and valuable content rather than wasted computational power.

Ahmiyat Coin

The native cryptocurrency of the network, Ahmiyat Coin (AHM), is awarded to memory contributors based on the quality and uniqueness of their uploads.

Technical Specifications

  • Consensus Algorithm: Proof of Memories
  • Block Time: ~10 minutes
  • Total Supply: 21,000,000 AHM
  • Memory Categories: Images, Videos, Memes, Text
  • Implementation: C++ with cryptographically secure functions
